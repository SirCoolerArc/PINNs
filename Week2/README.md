# Week 2 — Neural Networks from Scratch

## Topics covered
Building a neural network from first principles — layers, activations, loss functions,
and backpropagation — following the *Neural Networks from Scratch* book by Harrison
Kinsley & Daniel Kukieła. I completed through chapter 10 with a thorough understanding.

## Contents
- `NNFS.ipynb` — the main implementation, following the book chapter by chapter.
- `BasicNeuralNetwork.py`, `Loss.py`, `LearningRateDecay.py` — standalone experiments
  written to test individual pieces in isolation.

## Notes
Not every line from the book is reproduced, but the core mechanics are all here. To
solidify backpropagation I also watched additional videos and re-derived the gradient flow
myself. This from-scratch understanding is what makes the automatic-differentiation-based
PINN in Week 4 feel natural rather than magical.

## Resource
*Neural Networks from Scratch* — Harrison Kinsley & Daniel Kukieła.
